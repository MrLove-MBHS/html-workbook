Challenge 2: Tables

Write a Table for Yourself and 3 Friends

  • Use only <table>, <tr>, and <td> tags.
  • The table must have headings: Name, Age, Hair Colour (these go in the first row).
  • List yourself and three friends (made-up info is OK).
  • No other tags are allowed.

Here is an example (do not copy, use your own info):

Name Age Hair Colour
Sam 14 Brown
Jess 14 Blonde
Alex 15 Black
Mia 13 Red
